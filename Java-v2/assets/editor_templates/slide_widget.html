Sample Slide Widget

transition-effect:fade,transition-speed:400,display-length:6000

Instructions:

Create as many slides as you need by clicking the "Add New Empty Slide" button in the tool-bar above.

To add content in the slide, click on the slide (in some browsers, you may need to click twice, once to select the slide, and again to get a blinking cursor in the slide).

Instructions: (page 2)

At the top of the slides, you will notice a comma delimited list of settings.  These allow you to control advanced settings for the slide show, like transition effect (fade, slideX, slideY, etc.), the time the transition effect will last, in milliseconds, and the time each slide will be displayed, in milliseconds.  

You can also add other options like "display_navigation:true" and "display_progress:true" to allow the slideshow to be navigated, paused, etc. 

Instructions: (page 3)

available transition effects:

blindX, blindY, blindZ, cover, curtainX, curtainY, fade, fadeZoom, growX, growY, scrollUp, scrollDown, scrollLeft, scrollRight, scrollHorz, scrollVert, shuffle, slideX, slideY, toss, turnUp, turnDown, turnLeft, turnRight, uncover, wipe, zoom
